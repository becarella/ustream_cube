ustream_cube by becarella

A cube shows a UStream channel on each side. Only the top most plane plays video at any time, the rest are paused. It uses native Flash 10 and the UStream Flash Client API.

To use:


  • Click and drag the cube to rotate it (you may have to drag from the top or bottom of a side if you can't drag the video)
  • Right-click and choose a channel
  • Double click a side to have it face the camera squarely
  • Set your own channels by changing the comma-separated channel ids (cids) in the url:
    index.html?cids=1041782,1507098,440966,965158,43162,1909916

Known issues:


  • You may have to click above or below the video to drag if you can't drag from the video directly
  • Ads play to the end even if you turn away, so you may hear audio from 2 sources
